About

Rigorous Data Management
in Complex Development Environments

I am a monitoring, data management, and programme support professional with over five years of experience in internationally-funded development programmes. My work centres on designing and operating structured tracking systems, maintaining rigorous records, and producing the dashboards and status reports that donor organisations and programme leadership rely on to make evidence-based decisions.

My career spans grant portfolio management under IFAD and World Bank frameworks at SADP II, data operations at VFS Global, and agribusiness management at Sylrose Hatchery — each role demanding high accuracy, disciplined version control, and the ability to coordinate information across complex, multi-stakeholder environments.

I hold an MSc in Agricultural Resource Economics (Merit) from the National University of Lesotho and a Graduate Certificate from ESMT Berlin, where I trained in Data Analytics, Power BI, Machine Learning, and Storytelling with Data — giving me both the field credibility and the technical toolkit to drive institutional data management in development contexts.
